# Understanding-the-Evolution-of-the-Gaming-Industry-A-Data-Driven-Analysis-Using-Power-BI
This project explores the transformation of the gaming industry using data analytics and visualization techniques. 

**Project Objectives
Analyze global video game sales across platforms and genres
Identify top-performing developers and revenue concentration
Understand platform-wise trends (Console, PC, Handheld)
Examine shifts in the gaming industry post-2010
Derive actionable business insights and recommendations

**Tools & Technologies
Power BI – Dashboard creation and visualization
Microsoft Excel – Data cleaning and preprocessing

**Key Insights
Console gaming dominates total sales, driven by strong ecosystems and exclusive titles
Action, Sports, and Shooter genres contribute the highest revenue
Revenue is highly concentrated among a few leading developers such as Capcom
Peak sales were observed around 2020, followed by a gradual decline
PC gaming shows steady growth, while handheld platforms indicate niche potential

**Business Recommendations
Focus on multi-platform release strategies to maximize reach
Invest in high-performing genres and franchise development
Diversify developer partnerships to reduce dependency risks
Adopt subscription-based models like Xbox Game Pass
Extend product lifecycle through live services and downloadable content
